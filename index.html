Summary Overview

The Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) offers certifications and classes to help organizations implement agile practices at scale, improving productivity and efficiency.

Companies benefit from using SAFe by enabling alignment, collaboration, and delivery across multiple agile teams, leading to faster time-to-market and higher quality products.
